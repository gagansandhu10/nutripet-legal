# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** April 25, 2026

NutriPet (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates a mobile application that helps users track pet feeding and weight management.

We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### a) Account Information

* Email address
* Authentication data (via Supabase)

### b) Pet Information

* Pet name, species, weight, age, breed (optional)
* Feeding logs and weight tracking data

### c) Usage Data

* App interactions
* Feature usage
* Device information (non-identifiable)

### d) Payment Information

* Subscription data handled by Apple’s in-app purchase system and/or RevenueCat
* We do not store credit card details

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

* Provide and improve the app
* Personalise feeding and weight insights
* Generate AI-based recommendations
* Manage subscriptions
* Ensure security and prevent abuse

---

## 3. AI-Generated Content

NutriPet uses AI to provide general feeding and weight insights.

**Important:**

* AI outputs are for informational purposes only
* They are not veterinary advice
* You should consult a qualified veterinarian for medical concerns

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share data with:

* Service providers (Supabase, backend hosting)
* AI providers (for generating insights)
* Payment processors (Apple, RevenueCat)

These providers only process data necessary to perform their services.

---

## 5. Data Storage and Security

* Data is stored securely using industry-standard practices
* Access is restricted and authenticated
* We use encryption where appropriate

---
